Topic: Resolution

This semester, we will study the resolution proof system. It is a classical proof system, and one of a few propositional proof systems for which we have a solid understanding of its proof size bounds. We will first cover basic topics in general propositional proof complexity and later move on to resolution proof-size lower and upper bounds.

The program of the past semesters can be found at professor Krajíček's website.
